About

Painting in the Latent Space is a groundbreaking art project that bridges the gap between technology and traditional art. By leveraging custom plotter hardware and algorithmic techniques, each artwork transforms hidden mathematical patterns into visually stunning pieces. This human–machine collaboration results in a unique blend of digital precision and artisanal craftsmanship, inviting you to explore the infinite creative possibilities hidden within data.
